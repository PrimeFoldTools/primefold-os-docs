## The 2 Metrics That Matter

1. **FREE -> PAID CONVERSION %**
   - <5% = Problem (wrong audience)
   - 5-10% = Acceptable (optimize)
   - 10%+ = Good (safe to scale)
   - 20%+ = Exceptional

2. **MRR PER PRODUCT**
   - <$500 = Experimental (validate or kill)
   - $500+ = Proven (optimize)
   - $2K+ = Scaling (expand)
   - $5K+ = Mature (platform upgrade)
